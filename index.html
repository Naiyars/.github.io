ABOUT ME

I’m Naiyar Shaman, an engineer focused on building systems that have to function under real constraints — physical, operational, and environmental.

My work combines mechanical design, manufacturing, and aerospace systems architecture, with experience leading multidisciplinary efforts and developing mission-level concepts through NASA programs.
