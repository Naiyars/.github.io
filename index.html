PROJECT AUREVO — LUNAR SURFACE POWER ARCHITECTURE

Project AUREVO stands for Advanced Utilization of Resources for Energy & Viability Off-Earth. I founded and led Embry-Riddle’s NASA RASC-AL team to develop a technically rigorous lunar surface power concept, advancing the project through finalist selection with a systems-driven architecture supported by detailed analysis and engineering trade studies.

Lunar power architecture overview.

A 100 kWe hybrid surface power system integrating dual fission reactors, regolith thermal energy storage, and long-distance HVDC transmission for resilient lunar surface operations.
